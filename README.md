# 🏏 IPL Strategic Analytics Dashboard
A data-driven deep dive into the Indian Premier League (2008-2024) to analyze the impact of match conditions and toss decisions on team success.

## 📊 Project Overview
This project combines **Python-based data engineering** with **Tableau business intelligence** to uncover patterns in IPL match history. The goal was to determine if winning the toss provides a significant statistical advantage and to rank team performance over 16+ seasons.

## 💡 Key Insights
* **Toss Impact:** Analysis revealed a near-equal split in win-loss ratios following a toss win, suggesting that match-day conditions and venue history are stronger predictors of success than the toss alone.
* **Team Dominance:** Identified top-performing franchises based on total match wins, with a focus on historical consistency.
* **Venue Analysis:** (If you included this) Highlighted high-scoring venues vs. bowler-friendly pitches.
